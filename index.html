Summary: Healthcare vs Poverty Rates

The data set included with the assignment is based on 2014 ACS 1-year estimates: https://factfinder.census.gov/faces/nav/jsf/pages/searchresults.xhtml, but you are free to investigate a different data set. The current data set incldes data on rates of income, obesity, poverty, etc. by state. MOE stands for "margin of error."

Correlations Discovered Healthcare Access and Povety Level

We checked potential correlations between healthcare and poverty level among US States. Results suggest a positive association, that is, US States with higher poverty level also lack healthcare access (higher percentage lacking healthcare).
